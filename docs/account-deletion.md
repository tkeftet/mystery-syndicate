# Account & Data Deletion — Mystery Syndicate

**Last updated: July 6, 2026**

This page explains how to delete your Mystery Syndicate account and the data
associated with it. It applies to the Mystery Syndicate mobile game
(package `com.doomedgamesstudio.mysterysyndicate`).

## Delete your account from inside the app

The fastest way to permanently delete your account and data:

1. Open **Mystery Syndicate**.
2. Go to **Profile → Settings → Privacy Settings**.
3. Tap **Delete Account** and confirm.

Your account is deleted immediately and this action cannot be undone.

## Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app (for example, you uninstalled it or lost
access to your account), email us and we will delete your account and data on
your behalf:

**Contact:** [bakhti.oussamaa@gmail.com](mailto:bakhti.oussamaa@gmail.com)

Please send the request from the email address associated with your account, or
include your in-game username, so we can locate and verify the account. We aim
to process deletion requests within 30 days.

## What is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the personal data linked to
it, including:

- Your account record (email, username, and authentication credentials)
- Your gameplay progress (cases solved, scores, streaks, level, coins)
- Your season and event participation and leaderboard entries
- Your friends list and social connections
- Cosmetic and reward ownership tied to your account

## What may be retained

We may retain a limited amount of information where required to:

- Comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

Any retained data is kept only as long as necessary for these purposes and is
then deleted. Aggregated or anonymized data that can no longer be linked to you
may be retained.

For more detail on what we collect and why, see our
[Privacy Policy](https://tkeftet.github.io/mystery-syndicate/privacy-policy.html).

# Privacy Policy — Mystery Syndicate

**Last updated: June 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Mystery Syndicate ("we", "us", "the app") collects,
uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game. By using Mystery
Syndicate, you agree to this policy.

> **Contact:** [bakhti.oussamaa@gmail.com]
> Replace this with the email you want users and the app stores to contact you at.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Account information.** When you create an account we store a username and,
optionally, an email address and a securely hashed password. You may also play
as a guest, in which case we create an anonymous account identifier without an
email.

**Gameplay and profile data.** We store your in-game progress and activity,
including level, XP, coins, hints, streaks, solved cases, achievements,
collection/cosmetic items, season progress, daily-login history, friends,
detective-agency membership, leaderboard standing, and profile likes/views.

**Device and technical data.** To deliver core features we process technical
information such as device type, operating system, app version, language, a
session identifier, and IP address (used transiently for network requests and
abuse prevention).

**Push notification token.** If you enable notifications, we store a push token
(provided by Expo/Google/Apple) so we can send you gameplay reminders.

**Advertising data.** We show opt-in rewarded video ads through Google AdMob.
Depending on your consent choices, AdMob may access your device advertising
identifier and related data to serve and measure ads. See Section 4.

**Crash and diagnostic data.** We use Sentry to capture crashes and errors,
which may include device information, OS version, a stack trace, and your account
identifier, to help us fix bugs.

**Analytics data.** We collect anonymous, event-level analytics (e.g., case
started/completed, daily-login claimed, ad viewed) to understand how the game is
used and to improve it.

We do **not** knowingly collect precise location, contacts, photos, or any
special-category personal data.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to: operate and maintain your account and progress;
provide gameplay, social, and reward features; send notifications you opt into;
display ads; prevent cheating, fraud, and abuse; diagnose crashes and improve
performance; and comply with legal obligations.

## 3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with service
providers that help us run the app, namely:

- **Google AdMob / Google** — advertising and consent management (UMP).
- **Sentry** — crash and error monitoring.
- **Expo / Google (FCM) / Apple (APNs)** — push-notification delivery.
- **MongoDB Atlas** — database hosting for your account and game data.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy
policies. We may also disclose information if required by law.

## 4. Advertising & Your Consent Choices

We use Google AdMob for rewarded ads. For users in the European Economic Area,
the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we present a Google-certified consent
message (Google UMP) before serving personalized ads. If you do not consent, you
will receive non-personalized ads instead. You can change your choice at any time
in **Profile → Privacy → Manage ad privacy** inside the app.

Learn how Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## 5. Data Retention

We keep your account and game data for as long as your account exists. Crash and
analytics data are retained for a limited period per our providers' policies.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., under GDPR or U.S. state privacy laws) you may
have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw
consent. You can **delete your account and associated data from within the app**,
or contact us at [bakhti.oussamaa@gmail.com] and we will respond within a reasonable
time.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Mystery Syndicate is not directed to children under 13 (or under the minimum age of
digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from
children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will
delete it.

## 8. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures, including hashed passwords,
encrypted connections (TLS), and access controls. No method of transmission or
storage is 100% secure.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last
updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

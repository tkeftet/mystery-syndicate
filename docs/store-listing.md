### Feature graphic (the 1024×500 banner)
Create in Canva/Figma (free). Suggested layout:
- Dark background matching the app (near-black `#0D0D0F`) with an amber/gold accent (`#D4A84B`).
- Left: the app title "MYSTERY SYNDICATE" + tagline "A new case every day."
- Right: a moody detective motif (magnifying glass, case file, silhouette).
- Keep text away from the far edges (Play may crop).

### Screenshots to capture (aim for 5–6, in this order)
1. **Home** — today's case card + streak/XP.
2. **Investigation — Evidence tab** (shows the clue cards + progress bar).
3. **Investigation — Suspects tab** (the cast).
4. **Accusation modal** (picking the culprit — the tense moment).
5. **Result popup** — a "Case Solved" / Perfect Investigation win.
6. **Leaderboard** or **Season/Story map** (shows depth).

Add a short caption bar to each (in Canva) like: "Solve a new case daily", "Inspect every clue", "Name the culprit", "Climb the ranks". Optional but boosts conversion.

### How to capture screenshots
- Run the app on a phone (the preview build, or Expo Go), navigate to each screen, and take a device screenshot.
- Or run the Android emulator and screenshot there.
- Phone bezels/frames are optional; Play accepts raw screenshots.
